More about Me

As you may or may not have read, I moved around a lot growing up. I made friends everywhere but ended up losing connection with most of them, being how young we were. Growing up I was very outgoing, I met many people through clubs and sports. In addition, I've always played videogames so I made quite a few friends through that outlet.
My earliest memory of playing videogames is playing Pokèmon Fire Red on the GameBoy Advance. We also had a PS2 where I would be handed a controller that is not plugged in, and thought I was playing a golfing game with my father.
Since I owned my first GameBoy, I have had every generation of DS that has come after that. In addition, I had many other Nintendo consoles, Xbox's, PlayStation's, and most recently a gaming PC.
I do love the big beefy open world games such as Skyrim, Red Dead 2, or Breath of the Wild. Some of the recent simulation games also catch my eye, like Sims 4, Animal Shelter, and House Flipper. Then there's always those feel good games that hold a special place inside you, some of those games would be Pokèmon, Fire Emblem, Smash Brothers, Ark, Overwatch, and a few other smaller games.
