Randomized Pokemon Batlle Sim

This was my graduation project, where I was given complete freedom to create anything I wanted. Growing up, I played a lot of browser and flash games, so I wanted to create a simple game I could play on a website. I plan on expanding onto it since I have many ideas I could branch off of, but for the time being I have a working application with a simple battle mechanic. To set the scene, my website can do the following:

  • View all playable Pokemon in the application
  • Create a randomized party of 6
  • Create an NPC with 6 random Pokemon
  • Battle against a single random Pokemon encounter
  • And Battle! Using a simplified formula, but still implementing 'type matchups'
Eventually I want to add storyline progression, evolutions, EXP and level-ups, and more! In the meantime though, feel free to give it a shot and send me any feedback I could use for improvements!

Taco Bell!

Here we have one of my school assignments, where we were tasked to create a program to find which two Taco Bell's in the US were the furthest away from each other. We were provided with the locations and blank methods to fill in. We worked in groups of four and completed the assignment with little outside assistance.
